📝 How to Use TapSpeak

  1. Tap the "TAP" button: Press and hold it.
  2. Short Tap = Dot (·) — press quickly (less than 300ms).
  3. Long Tap = Dash (–) — press longer (300ms or more).
  4. Pause 1 second between letters to auto-convert.
  5. Text Output shows your decoded message.
  6. Click 🔊 Speak to hear the message spoken aloud.
  7. Use shortcut buttons for common emergency words.
  8. Click 📘 Morse Code Guide to view all Morse codes.
